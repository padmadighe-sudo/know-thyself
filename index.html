🔢 Lo Shu Numerology Grid

The Lo Shu Grid (also called Magic Square or Numerology Talisman) is an ancient Chinese numerology system that maps the digits from your birth date onto a 3×3 grid. It reveals your strengths, weaknesses, and life lessons.

📖 How to Read Your Grid

Numbers with dots (●): These numbers appear in your birth date and represent natural abilities and traits you possess.

Multiple dots: When a number appears multiple times in your birth date, it indicates a very strong influence of that energy in your life.

Empty cells (missing numbers): These represent qualities you need to develop through life experiences - your karmic lessons.

Planes & Arrows: When three numbers align horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, they form "planes" or "arrows" that reveal special characteristics and patterns in your personality.

💡 Tip: The Lo Shu Grid is used in Chinese metaphysics and Pythagorean numerology. Many practitioners create a physical talisman of their grid and carry it as a symbolic charm for manifestation and self-awareness.
